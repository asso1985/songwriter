# Brainstorming Session Results

**Facilitator:** Omar
**Date:** 2026-04-13

## Session Overview

**Topic:** MVP feature ideation for Songwriter — a visual, interactive chord navigation tool with AI music theory guidance
**Goals:** Generate a rich pool of feature ideas for the MVP

### Session Setup

The Songwriter app concept centers on a visual chord wheel/map that users click through to navigate chord progressions. At each chord, the app highlights "strong next moves" and provides AI-powered explanations of why each suggestion works — covering tension, resolution, modal color, and other music theory concepts.

## Technique Selection

**Approach:** Progressive Technique Flow
**Journey Design:** Systematic development from exploration to action

**Progressive Techniques:**

- **Phase 1 - Exploration:** SCAMPER Method for maximum idea generation across seven creative lenses

## Technique Execution Results

### Phase 1: SCAMPER — Expansive Exploration

**17 ideas generated across UI, structure, audio, and AI dimensions.**

#### All Ideas Generated

**[UI #1]**: Grid/Tile Layout
_Concept_: Chords displayed as a grid of tappable tiles — current chord highlighted, adjacent tiles glow to show strong next moves.
_Novelty_: More natural for touch interfaces, scales better across screen sizes.

**[UI #2]**: Path/Flow View
_Concept_: A horizontal/vertical "river" where your progression builds directionally. Each chord extends the path with branching options ahead.
_Novelty_: Makes the journey of the progression visible — where you've been and where you could go.

**[Audio #3]**: Inline Audio Preview
_Concept_: Every suggested chord has a play button — tap to hear how it sounds in context of your existing progression, not in isolation.
_Novelty_: Hearing the transition teaches your ear; explanation + audio closes the theory-to-ear gap.

**[UI #4]**: Interactive Chord Network Graph
_Concept_: Chords as nodes in a network where distance/thickness of connections represents harmonic "closeness." Current chord pulses, strong moves are visually closer and brighter, exotic moves are further and dimmer.
_Novelty_: The visual IS the theory — users intuitively grasp harmonic relationships without reading. Close = safe, distant = adventurous.

**[UI #5]**: Zoom Levels on the Graph
_Concept_: Zoomed in shows 3-5 strongest next moves. Zoomed out reveals the full harmonic landscape with visual weight showing common vs. rare paths.
_Novelty_: Beginners stay zoomed in, advanced users zoom out. Same interface, different skill levels, no mode-switching.

**[Audio #6]**: Contextual Playback Chain
_Concept_: Hovering/tapping a suggested chord plays the last 2-3 chords in your progression leading into the new one. You hear the actual transition in context.
_Novelty_: A chord's emotional impact depends on what came before. Contextual playback is what your ear actually needs.

**[AI #7]**: Relationship Labels on Edges
_Concept_: Connecting lines in the graph carry short labels — "resolves tension," "adds color," "surprise move." Tap the edge for the full AI explanation.
_Novelty_: Theory lives on the connections, not in a separate panel. Users learn by navigating.

**[Structure #8]**: Section-Based Song Builder
_Concept_: Top-level view shows song sections (Verse, Pre-Chorus, Chorus, Bridge, Special) as distinct blocks. Build a chord loop within each section; AI suggestions adapt based on which section you're in.
_Novelty_: Context-aware suggestions — the AI knows "you're on chord IV in a pre-chorus" and adjusts for building tension toward the chorus.

**[Structure #9]**: Section Transition Intelligence
_Concept_: When moving between sections, AI suggests chords that bridge them — "this chord lifts energy from your verse into the pre-chorus."
_Novelty_: Most tools treat sections in isolation. This understands transitions between sections are where songs succeed or fail.

**[Structure #10]**: Section Templates / Starting Points
_Concept_: Pick a section type and get a pre-loaded starting chord with the graph weighted for that section's role. Verse starts favor I or vi, chorus starts favor IV or V.
_Novelty_: Reduces blank-canvas paralysis with contextually intelligent defaults.

**[Playback #11]**: Full Song Playback Chain
_Concept_: Once loops are built for multiple sections, play the entire song structure — Verse → Pre-Chorus → Chorus — as one continuous piece.
_Novelty_: The moment it stops being "chord experiments" and becomes "my song." The emotional payoff feature.

**[Structure #12]**: Section Energy Curve
_Concept_: A visual arc above the song structure showing energy trajectory. As you pick chords, the curve updates to reflect whether choices match or subvert expected energy.
_Novelty_: Makes the invisible "feel" of a song visible without being prescriptive.

**[AI #13]**: Adaptive Explanation Depth Toggle (Flow/Learn Mode)
_Concept_: Toggle between "Flow Mode" (emoji cues — thumbs up for safe, fire for bold, sparkle for colorful) and "Learn Mode" (full theory explanations with terminology and examples).
_Novelty_: Same app serves "I'm writing right now" and "I want to understand why." Switch mid-session.

**[AI #14]**: Progressive Disclosure on Tap
_Concept_: In Flow Mode, suggestions show just an icon. Tap once for one-sentence summary. Tap again for full theory. Long-press for audio + explanation. Layers unfold per chord.
_Novelty_: User controls depth per chord, not globally. Go deep only where you're curious.

**[AI #15]**: Explanation Style Personas
_Concept_: AI voices tuned to instrument — "Explain like I'm a guitarist" (fret references), "like a pianist" (voice leading), "like a producer" (mood/vibe).
_Novelty_: Same theory, different framing. Instrument-aware explanations make theory immediately actionable.

**[AI #16]**: Contextual Micro-Lessons
_Concept_: When you make an interesting chord choice in Learn Mode, AI offers an optional 30-second micro-lesson: "You just used a deceptive cadence! Here's why it works." Dismissable card, never interrupts flow.
_Novelty_: Learning at the exact moment of relevance, embedded in creative choices.

**[AI #17]**: History-Aware Explanations
_Concept_: AI remembers what it's explained. First V→I? Full explanation. Tenth time? Just the emoji. V→I in a new context? Highlights what's different.
_Novelty_: The app gets smarter about you over time, not just about music theory.

### Phase 4: Resource Constraints — MVP Scoping

**Final MVP scope — 6 core features (all ship together):**

1. **Interactive Chord Network Graph** — nodes with distance = harmonic closeness
2. **Zoom Levels** — zoomed in for beginners, zoomed out for advanced
3. **Contextual Audio Playback** — hear transitions in context, not isolated chords
4. **Section-Based Song Builder** — verse, pre-chorus, chorus, bridge, special
5. **Full Song Playback** — hear all sections as one continuous piece
6. **Flow/Learn Mode Toggle** — emoji cues for writing, full theory for learning
